COMPANY OVERVIEW:-

Firgun Studios Private Limited is a Media Merchant Company. It was established in 2014, specializing in the acquisition of rights for movies/content across different states and languages. With a comprehensive range of services in linear, non-linear, remake, and music rights management. We are dedicated to sourcing and securing the distribution rights for a diverse range of content, enabling their availability to audiences in various regions and linguistic. We serve as a bridge between content creators and platforms, ensuring smooth transactions and maximizing the value of media assets. We have a dedicated team of professionals, with our expertise in Negotiating, Acquisition, Legal, Technical and Regional Expertise.

  • Firgun Studios deals with LINEAR, NON LINEAR, REMAKE RIGHTS & MUSIC RIGHTS:-
  • Linear Rights: - Linear Rights refer to the permissions and licenses required for traditional broadcast distribution, such as television networks and cable channels.
  • Non Linear Rights: - Non Linear Rights pertain to the licensing and distribution of content on digital platforms, including streaming services and video-on-demand platforms., such as Zee 5, Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, or Hulu, as well as transactional VOD platforms like iTunes or Google Play.
  • REMAKE RIGHTS: - Remake rights allow for the creation of new versions or adaptations of existing media properties, requiring legal permissions to reproduce and distribute the content with creative modifications
  • MUSIC: - Music rights involve obtaining licenses to use copyrighted music in media productions, encompassing both the composition rights (melody, lyrics, arrangement) and sound recording rights. Licensing music rights ensures legal compliance and compensates the rights holders for the use of their copyrighted works in films, TV shows, commercials, and other audiovisual projects.
